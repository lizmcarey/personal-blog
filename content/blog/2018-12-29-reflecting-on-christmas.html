A sign of getting older is when visiting home becomes more stressful, than not. With every year that passes, a responsbility is added and layered to your plate.

Holidays are no longer about about predicting what gifts I’ll receive, but rather headcount and logistics planning, including meal prep.

2018 Carey family Christmas menu:

  • Mixed salad
  • Roasted root vegetables (recipe from NYT Cooking)
  • Mashed potatoes
  • Beef tenderloin with horseradish crème fraîche sauce (recipe from Serious Eats )
  • Apple crisp

Christmas Eve Shopping tips:
Grocery shopping on Christmas eve is a depressing event: The shelves are empty, the store clerks don’t want to be there, and everyone currently shopping is in a state of panic and distress, desparately hoping to cross off the remaining items on their grocery lists.

Notes on the menu items:

  • Add quartered red onions to the root vegetables
  • Coring parsnips is nonsense, disregard those instructions
  • Use the horseradish crème fraîche recipe from NYT cooking, it’s less effort than Serious Eats
  • Horseradish is impossible to find on Christmas Eve, but ginger makes a great substitute!
  • Use an instant read thermometer to cook the beef to your liking (I went a bought a Thermapen MK4 for next year)
